OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

Build Club is proud to announce that our team Svayatt has qualified for the first round of the prestigious ISRO Robotics Challenge - URSC 2025. This reflects our team's dedication, technical skill, and passion for advancing space robotics.

For the challenge, we developed a highly capable autonomous drone designed for complex, mission-critical tasks. The project demanded deep research, hands-on engineering, and innovative problem-solving, integrating advanced sensors, control systems, and automation.

The journey involved long hours of design, testing, and iteration, powered by teamwork, resilience, and a shared vision. It was a transformative experience that strengthened both our technical expertise and collaborative spirit.

This achievement marks a major milestone for Build Club and inspires us to keep pushing the limits of innovation and excellence.
